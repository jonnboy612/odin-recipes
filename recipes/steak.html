Steak

This recipe is for a butter based steak.

Ingredients

  • 1-2" Ribeye
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • butter
  • Garlic
  • Thyme
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Take out steak at least a hour before cooking to get it to room temp for even cooking
  2. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Heat up pan until oil is smoking
  4. Lay steak on pay away from you
  5. Sear on each side for about 3 minutes or until a golden brown crust is formed
  6. After flipping turn down temperature and drop in butter
  7. Baste steak until it is cooked to your doneness
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before cutting and serving
