Scallops

This recipe is for a pan seared scallops.

Ingredients

  • Scallops
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • butter
  • Garlic
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat man with oil until smoking
  2. Season scallops with salt and pepper
  3. Once pan is smoking drop scallops into the pan leaving room inbetween
  4. Sear about a minute each side or until browned
  5. Turn down the heat and add your butter and lemon
  6. Let cook for another 2 minutes and serve
